Community

Goals

GermanEOS aims at being the largest EOS community in Germany. We promote the development of decentralized applications (DAPP) of EOS platform and other blockchain-based technology in the area of energy (SmartEnergy), finance (Coiner.world), logistics, and water.

Infrastructure

GermanEOS will provide class-leading infrastructures to support the main EOS network. Substantial efforts will be invested in protecting GermanEOS nodes from DDOS attacks.

Declaration

EOS coins distrbuted to GermanEOS will be fully used in community building and promotion of developing decentralized applications on top of EOS. GermanEOS will keep a minimum amount of the refunds for daily operations.

Campaign

GermanEOS is based in Bonn, Germany. The current computing infrastructure consists of one dedicated servers with 24 Xeon CPUs, 192 GB memory, and 500 GB SSD. The system will be scaled out automatically in the future depending on the loads of EOS main network.
